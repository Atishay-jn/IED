M~T Presents Modular Table

At Muffin Time, we realised how we often end up with messy wires and lousy cable management in our living rooms. As the dawn of IoT devices approaches, the average number of devices a person carries is expected double, and some estimates even say triple! Thus we have come up with an idea to help you solve it all without taking more space in your living room.

As there doesn't exist one size that fits all, we have created a modular table where our customers can choose what modules are relevant to them and can power them all with a single wire!
